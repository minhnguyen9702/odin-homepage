Solid Snake

About Me

"Hey, I'm Solid Snake. You might know me as a soldier, a covert operative, or the guy who’s had to save the world a few times. I specialize in infiltration, stealth, and taking down impossible threats—usually solo. I’ve spent my life on the frontlines, from dismantling rogue military factions to preventing nuclear disasters. Sure, it hasn’t been easy, but someone has to get the job done. Here, you’ll find more about my missions, my story, and what drives me. If you’re looking for a quiet life, you won’t find it here. But if you’re ready to face the truth, you’re in the right place."

My Missions

Operation Intrude N313

I was sent into Outer Heaven to stop a new threat: Metal Gear. My mission was simple—sneak in, destroy the mechanical beast, and rescue my comrade, Gray Fox. Every step was a test of my training and resolve, as I faced the mercenaries guarding the facility, relying on my stealth and combat skills.

Operation GANDER

My orders took me to Zanzibar Land, where I had to stop a new Metal Gear from being built. I infiltrated the enemy base, dodging guards and traps. I had to rescue hostages, including Dr. Kio Marv, while piecing together the enemy's plans. It was a race against time, and failure wasn’t an option.

Shadow Moses Island

Infiltrating Shadow Moses Island was my most dangerous mission yet. Foxhound was in control, and I had to eliminate their leader, Liquid Snake, while preventing the launch of Metal Gear. I fought through soldiers, faced off against my former comrades, and endured personal betrayals. My resolve was tested at every turn, but I would not let the world fall into chaos.

Big Shell Incident

I wasn’t the one in the field this time; I operated from the shadows, guiding Raiden through a crisis at the Big Shell. I needed to uncover the truth behind the terrorist takeover while ensuring he didn’t get caught in their deadly web. My experience and strategy were crucial as I fed him information about the enemy's movements.

Operation Snake Eater

In this VR mission, I played as Naked Snake and was sent to the Soviet Union to eliminate my former mentor, The Boss. Using stealth in the jungle, I aimed to take down The Boss and the COBRA unit, retrieve the Philosopher's Legacy, and prevent a global catastrophe. Each encounter tested my beliefs about loyalty and sacrifice.

Operation Neutralize

Age had caught up with me, but the mission remained the same: stop Liquid Ocelot and his plan to control the world’s military systems. I traveled across war-torn landscapes, confronting old friends and enemies, reliving the pain of my past. My mission was not just about survival; it was about breaking the cycle of violence.
